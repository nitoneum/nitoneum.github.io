nitoneum

Nitoneum is an experimental digital currency that enables instant payments to anyone, anywhere in the world. Nitoneum uses peer-to-peer technology to operate with no central authority: managing transactions and issuing money are carried out collectively by the network. Nitoneum Core is the name of open source software which enables the use of this currency. nitoneum is currently in a very early stage and therefore the coins must be generated (mined).
