nitoneum

nitoneum is a peer-to-peer electronic cash system that enables instant payments to anyone in the world. it is based on the Litecoin Project, which means it's fast and have lower transaction fees. nitoneum is currently in a very early stage and therefore the coins must be generated (mined).
